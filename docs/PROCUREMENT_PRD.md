# Product Requirements Document: Procurement & Bidding

## 1. Executive Summary

The Procurement module digitizes the organization's tendering process. It allows the organization to publish opportunities (procurements) and enables pre-qualified contractors to submit bids digitally. This ensures a fair, transparent, and organized selection process.

## 2. Target Audience

- **Procurement Officers**: Staff who create tenders and manage the bidding process.
- **Contractors**: Pre-registered external vendors who bid for jobs.
- **Evaluation Committee**: The team responsible for reviewing bids and selecting winners.
- **Public**: General public viewing open opportunities (transparency).

## 3. Goals & Objectives

- **Transparency**: Make all open tenders publicly visible.
- **Fairness**: Ensure only verified, active contractors can bid.
- **Efficiency**: Replace paper bid submissions with a digital portal.
- **Compliance**: Enforce deadlines strictlyâ€”no late bids allowed.

## 4. Key Features

### 4.1. Contractor Registry

- **Verification**: A database of pre-approved contractors. Only those with a valid, active "Contractor Number" can participate.
- **Profile Data**: Stores company details, registration categories, and contact info.

### 4.2. Tender Management (Internal)

- **Create & Publish**: Admins can draft procurements with Title, Scope of Work, Budget, and Deadlines.
- **Document Distribution**: Attach necessary files (Request for Proposal, Technical Specs) for contractors to download.
- **Lifecycle Management**: Manage the status of a tender: Draft -> Published -> Closed -> Awarded -> Archived.

### 4.3. Bidding Portal (External)

- **Open Opportunities**: A public page listing all active procurements.
- **Secure Submission**: Contractors log in with their unique ID to submit bids.
- **Proposal Uploads**: Contractors can upload Technical and Commercial proposals securely.
- **Bid Management**: Contractors can update or withdraw their bids before the deadline.

### 4.4. Evaluation & Awarding

- **Bid Review**: Admins can view all submitted bids for a specific project in one place.
- **Status Tracking**: Mark bids as "Under Review", "Accepted", or "Rejected".
- **Awarding**: Formally select a winning bid and close the procurement.

## 5. User Stories

- _As a Procurement Officer_, I want to publish a tender for "Office Renovation" and attach the architectural drawings.
- _As a Contractor_, I want to browse open jobs and submit my quote and proposal documents online.
- _As a Contractor_, I want to update my pricing before the deadline because material costs changed.
- _As an Evaluator_, I want to see a list of all companies that bid for a project to compare their proposals.

## 6. Success Metrics

- Increased number of competitive bids per project.
- 100% compliance with submission deadlines (system enforced).
- Reduction in administrative time spent organizing paper bid documents.
