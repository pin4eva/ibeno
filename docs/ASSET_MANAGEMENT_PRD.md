# Product Requirements Document: Asset Management

## 1. Executive Summary

The Asset Management module provides a digital registry for tracking the organization's physical assets. It replaces spreadsheets and manual logs with a centralized system that tracks what assets exist, where they are located, and what they look like, ensuring accountability and reducing loss.

## 2. Target Audience

- **Asset Managers**: Staff responsible for procurement and inventory control.
- **Facility Managers**: Staff overseeing specific locations (offices, sites).
- **Auditors**: Internal or external reviewers checking company property.

## 3. Goals & Objectives

- **Accountability**: Know exactly what assets the organization owns and where they are.
- **Organization**: Standardize how assets are categorized and named.
- **Visual Verification**: Keep photographic records of assets to monitor condition and identity.

## 4. Key Features

### 4.1. Asset Registry

- **Central Database**: A single source of truth for all furniture, electronics, vehicles, and equipment.
- **Detailed Records**: Track essential details including Name, Description, and Asset Type.
- **Auto-Numbering**: The system automatically assigns a unique ID (e.g., `AST-2025-001`) to every new asset to prevent duplicates.

### 4.2. Location Tracking

- **Location Assignment**: Assign every asset to a specific physical location (e.g., "Head Office", "Site A").
- **Filtering**: Quickly view all assets at a specific location for audits.

### 4.3. Visual Management

- **Image Gallery**: Upload photos of assets to verify their condition and identity.
- **Cloud Storage**: Images are stored securely in the cloud for easy access from anywhere.

### 4.4. Search & Discovery

- **Smart Search**: Find assets instantly by typing their name or unique asset number.
- **Categorization**: Filter assets by type (e.g., "Laptop", "Generator", "Furniture") to analyze inventory.

## 5. User Stories

- _As an Asset Manager_, I want to register a new batch of laptops and have the system generate unique tags for them.
- _As a Facility Manager_, I want to see a list of all assets assigned to my branch to conduct a monthly audit.
- _As an Auditor_, I want to search for a specific generator by its tag number to verify its purchase date and details.

## 6. Success Metrics

- 100% of physical assets tagged and registered in the system.
- Reduction in "lost" or "unaccounted for" items during annual audits.
- Reduced time to locate specific equipment details.
